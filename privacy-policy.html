1. Overview

Midnight Anime ("we," "our," "the Service") is a free anime discovery and streaming-companion web app. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use the Service, how we use it, and the choices available to you.

We built Midnight Anime as an independent project. We are not affiliated with AniList, The Movie Database (TMDB), Firebase/Google, or any of the third-party embed providers referenced below — each operates under its own separate privacy policy, which we link to where relevant.

2. Information We Collect

Account information

If you create an account, we collect the information you provide directly:

  • Email & password (if you sign up with email) — your password is never stored or seen by us; it is handled entirely by Google Firebase Authentication.
  • Name, email, and profile photo (if you sign in with Google) — provided to us by Google after you grant permission during sign-in.

Usage data you generate

  • Watchlist — anime you've saved.
  • Watch progress — which episode and roughly how far into it you were, so "Continue Watching" works.
  • Episode ratings — likes/dislikes you submit on episodes, which are stored publicly (visible to all users as aggregate counts) alongside your private per-episode vote.
  • Avatar choice — if you pick a profile picture from our avatar gallery (sourced from nekos.best), we store which image you selected.
  • Notification preference — whether you've enabled browser notifications for new episode releases, and which anime you've already been notified about.

Information we do not collect

We do not collect payment information (the Service is free), government ID, precise location, or biometric data. We do not run analytics/tracking scripts (e.g. Google Analytics, Meta Pixel) at the time of writing.

4. Third-Party Video Sources

Important: Episode playback is provided through embedded third-party video players. We do not host, store, upload, or control the video files themselves.

When you press play, your browser loads a video player inside an embedded frame hosted by a separate provider. That provider — not Midnight Anime — receives your IP address, browser information, and playback requests directly, and may serve its own advertising or set its own cookies within that embedded frame. We do not control, and are not responsible for, that provider's data practices. We recommend reviewing the relevant provider's own policy if you have concerns, or using browser privacy tools if you'd prefer they not see your activity.

5. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate core features: sign-in, watchlist, continue watching, ratings.
  • To personalize your experience (e.g. showing your avatar, resuming episodes).
  • To send browser notifications you've explicitly opted into, about new episode releases for anime on your watchlist.
  • To maintain and improve the Service (bug fixing, performance).

We do not sell your personal information to advertisers or data brokers.

6. Data Storage & Security

Account and usage data is stored in Google Firebase's Realtime Database. Access is restricted so that only you (authenticated as yourself) can read or write your own private data (watchlist, progress, personal rating record); episode like/dislike totals are public by design, similar to how many streaming platforms display aggregate rating counts.

No online service can guarantee perfect security. We take reasonable, industry-standard steps (Firebase's built-in security rules and encryption in transit) to protect your data, but cannot guarantee absolute security against sophisticated attacks.

7. Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We may disclose information if required to do so by law, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Midnight Anime, our users, or the public.

8. Cookies & Local Storage

We use browser local storage (not third-party tracking cookies) to remember your recent searches and basic preferences on your device. Firebase Authentication may set its own cookies to keep you signed in. Third-party video embeds (see Section 4) may set their own cookies, which we do not control.

9. Your Choices & Rights

  • Access/delete your data: sign in and use the app's settings to remove items from your watchlist; to delete your account entirely, contact us (Section 12).
  • Notifications: disable browser notifications any time via the toggle in Settings, or through your browser's own site-permission controls.
  • Sign out: available any time from Settings.

10. Children's Privacy

Midnight Anime is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete it.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the Service after changes take effect means you accept the revised policy.

12. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy? Reach out via the app's Settings page, or through our hosting project contact channel.
